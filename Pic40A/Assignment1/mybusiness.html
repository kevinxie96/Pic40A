Company History

This company represents an amalgamation of hobbies. It was conceived on the eve of my first day of 7th grade. I realized that geeks and nerds have a common personality but they rarely associated with eachother. That is what makes KPCBL so special. It brings together two diverse minorities in society, uniting them to fight back against the jocks who jeer them everyday. In addition, KPCBL offers the cheapest prices for the best quality products.

Lastly, for you super-introverts, you can still keep to yourself as half the library as been sectioned off by sound proof glass. For the riotous gamers who come to KPCBL, please enjoy but be considerate: it is still a library.
